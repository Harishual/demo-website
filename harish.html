Why kerala So Special

Kerala is a beautiful state in India. It is found at the southern tip of our country. Lots of coconut trees grow here. The state has many rivers and beaches. People in Kerala love to dance and they have a special dance called Kathakali. They speak the Malayalam language. You will find many colorful birds and animals in Kerala, like the Indian elephant. They also have a big festival called Onam. During Onam, people wear new clothes and enjoy a big meal. Kerala is also known for its houseboats and hill stations like Munnar. It’s a wonderful place to visit.
